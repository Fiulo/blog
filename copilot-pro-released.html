Copilot Pro Released

Copilot Pro is a premium subscription offering that enhances your productivity with priority access to GPT-4 and GPT-4 Turbo, faster AI image creation, and additional features. It is available for educational faculty and staff as a paid add-on for Microsoft 365 A3/A5 and Office 365 A3/A5.

Purpose and target audience

The target audience for Copilot Pro includes individuals who want to boost their creativity and productivity using AI-powered features. The subscription is designed for personal and family users who are already subscribed to Microsoft 365 or Office 365.

Release and Availability

Copilot Pro was launched on January 15, 2024, and is priced at $20 per month per user. It is available as a paid add-on for existing subscribers of Microsoft 365 A3/A5 and Office 365 A3/A5.

Key Features

Copilot Pro offers a range of features that go beyond the standard Copilot experience:

  1. Single AI experience across devices: The AI experience is consistent across devices, understanding your context on the web, on your PC, and across your apps.
  2. Customizable Copilot GPTs: Users can customize the behavior of Copilot on specific topics.
  3. Integration with Office software: Copilot Pro integrates with Excel, PowerPoint, and Word, allowing users to draft documents, summarize emails, create presentations, and more.
  4. Access to Copilot GPT Builder: This feature is not mentioned in the provided search results, but it is listed as a key feature in the question.
  5. Search results: The search results provided do not directly address this feature, but they do discuss other features and aspects of Copilot Pro.

Copilot Pro subscribers can access the service via the Copilot app on the web, in select Microsoft 365 apps, and on platforms such as Windows and mobile devices.

Copilot Mobile App

The Copilot Mobile App is now available for both Android and iOS devices, allowing users to access the power of Copilot on the go.

Copilot in Microsoft 365 Mobile App

Copilot Pro brings advanced features and capabilities to users across their devices, providing seamless integration with the app for Android and iOS.

Integration with the App for Android and iOS

The Copilot app connects users to GPT-4 and Dall-E 3, enabling them to sync chats and queries from their phone to their Windows PC.

Comparison with Other Copilot Offerings

Copilot Pro vs. Copilot Azure

Copilot Pro is a premium subscription for individuals that provides a higher tier of service for AI, while Copilot Azure is designed for customer service use cases and integrates with customer relationship management (CRM) software.

Copilot Pro vs. Copilot for Microsoft 365

Copilot Pro is a premium subscription for personal use, while Copilot for Microsoft 365 is designed for businesses and can be extended and customized to fit specific organizational needs.

Conclusion

The Copilot Mobile App offers a convenient way for users to access the power of Copilot on their Android and iOS devices. With seamless integration and advanced features, it provides users with a powerful AI companion on the go.
